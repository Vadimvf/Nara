Nara

The year is 1985. A textile designer has fallen asleep playing Space Invaders on the Nintendo Entertainment System. Thanks to the tetris effect and 80's designers' obessions for bright geometric fabrics the dream is dominated by invading technicolor textile designs.

How to Play

Avoid textiles of the opposite color, collect those of the same color for health and to fill up your power up meter. Adjust your window size so you can see the button below.

Window is too small Ready!

Controls

  • Use your arrow keys to move
  • Space to shoot
  • F to toggle your color
  • D to use your powerup
  • Click Here to Train!
